Quick Help
How do I track my order?
Once your order is shipped, you'll receive a tracking link via email. You can also track it from My Orders in your account.
What is your return policy?
We offer hassle-free returns within 30 days of delivery. The item must be unused and in its original packaging. Refunds are processed within 5–7 business days.
How do I cancel an order?
Orders can be cancelled within 24 hours of placing them. Go to My Orders, select the order, and click Cancel. After 24 hours, please contact our support team.
Do you offer international shipping?
Currently we ship within India only. We are working on expanding to international destinations — stay tuned for updates!
