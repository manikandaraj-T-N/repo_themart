How We Protect Your Payment

Security is our top priority. Here's how we keep every transaction safe:

  • All pages on TheMart use HTTPS with 256-bit SSL encryption.
  • Card details are tokenized and never stored on our servers.
  • Payments are processed through PCI DSS Level 1 certified gateways.
  • Two-factor authentication is available for all registered accounts.
  • Real-time fraud monitoring detects suspicious transactions automatically.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is it safe to save my card on TheMart?

Yes. We use tokenization — your actual card number is never stored on our servers. Only a secure token is saved for future purchases.

What should I do if my payment fails?

Check your card details and ensure sufficient funds are available. If the issue persists, try a different payment method or contact your bank. You can also reach our support team.

When will my refund be credited?

Refunds are typically processed within 7–10 business days after we receive the returned item. The time to reflect in your account depends on your bank or payment provider.

Are my transactions monitored for fraud?

Yes. Our advanced fraud detection systems monitor all transactions in real time. If anything suspicious is detected, we will contact you immediately and may temporarily hold the transaction for your protection.
